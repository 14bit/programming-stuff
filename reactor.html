Reactor 4CP Steel-Style

Personal Mapping Project

Reactor is a map for the game Team Fortress 2 that I began work on in September of 2015. It was originally designed for the TF2maps.net Dynamic Control Point Contest. I designed the layout and gameplay, and a user known as Corvatile stepped up to do the artpass of the map. When the contest ended, it somehow managed to get 5th place. Since then I have repeatedly attempted to continue work on the map, but I never could figure out how to fix the major player flow issues and would give up each time. However, I recently had a breakthrough and have begin major renovations of the map.
